Can I make changes to a pending reservation request?

Yes. If the request hasn’t been accepted by the host, you can cancel the request and send a new one with the updated reservation details.

To change a reservation request:

  1. Go to Trips on airbnb.com
  2. Find the pending reservation request you want to cancel
  3. Click Cancel Request and then OK to confirm
  4. If you want, send a new request with your changed dates or number of guests.

If you choose this option, the money you paid towards your initial reservation is immediately available to use towards a new reservation.

If you choose this option, the money you paid towards your initial reservation is immediately available to use towards a new reservation. Once you've found a new place, we'll automatically transfer your original payment to your new reservation request, along with any eligible credit from Airbnb.

If you don't want to book another place on Airbnb, you can ask for a full refund. To get a refund:

  • In the Airbnb app, tap Trips Airbnb logo and choose a trip
  • Find your canceled reservation and tap to view the itinerary

Note: In the event that your reservation is more than 4 weeks away, your payment will be automatically refunded.
